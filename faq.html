What is Energy Trends Australia?

We’re a subscription-based energy intelligence platform that helps commercial and industrial users reduce procurement risk and build long-range electricity plans with clarity and confidence.

Who is it built for?

Large energy users across NSW, VIC, QLD, and SA who need strategic insights—not guesswork—when navigating complex electricity markets.

Do I need deep market knowledge to use the tools?

No. We’ve designed our tools to deliver actionable outputs. You’ll get clarity, not complexity.

Is Energy Pulse a forecast?

No. Energy Pulse provides signals to help guide timing and strategy—it’s a strategic pulse check, not a crystal ball.

How can I access the subscriber-only tools?

We’re currently preparing our secure portal for role-based login and MFA protection. Stay tuned or reach out for early access details.
